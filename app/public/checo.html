CHECO

PASSION • INSPIRATION • DETERMINATION

Checo is a remarkably zealous & fanatical Master Barber who is instilled with great passion & love for the profession of Hair Styling. A co-founder of “Stache Barbers” and a licensed cosmetologist, he currently resides in Union, New Jersey and is an innovative professional barber who loves practicing ingenuity & creativity. Checo grew up in a barbershop atmosphere and witnessing the exceptional work of his partner Chaz inspired him to embark on a professional journey as a Master Barber. Learning the pearls of hair cutting & hair styling from the very hands of his mentor Chaz enabled him to serve the world of fashion with an incredible blend of professionalism & enthusiasm. Checo has had the luxury of working with several renowned Barbering brands and has worked along with Wahl, Andis, Sexy Hair, Xotics, Babyliss Pro. His work has been featured on XXL Magazine, Sean John Clothings, Enyce Clothing and has also worked on a Men’s product Development team for L’Oréal. An avid seeker of knowledge, he encourages the idea of evolving as a professional and is always on the lookout for an opportunity to learn and explore new passing skills each day. Checo serves as an ambassador of vibrant hair cutting & hair styling trends and loves inspiring & motivating the passionate barbers of today & tomorrow. Previously, as a professional, Checo has won several barber battles, owns a T-Shirt Line and also had the luxury of serving celebrities like Snoop Dogg, DJ Khaled, Quincy and Sean “Diddy” Combs. Checo’s journey to professional career success started from the age of 9 and his love & passion for the profession has always been his constant driving force. From the depths of his basement, he had always been able to outshine and inspire others with his unique blend of skills & passion for hair styling. Checo’s passion inspired him to study cosmetology and enabled him to infuse the two together with an aim to serve the world of fashion with a brand new outlook and approach to hair styling. Checo’s primary objective is to develop a product line of his very own and be a source of inspiration for individuals who are inclined towards following the career path of professional barbers. He loves educating others about the tricks & traits of the art of barbering while also sharing a great love for his profession with them.Checo’s greatest pleasure in life comes from walking the career road he truly loves.
